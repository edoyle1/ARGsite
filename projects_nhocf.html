North Hill - Olin College Fund for Innovation in Aging

The North Hill - Olin College Fund for Innovation in Aging is a collaboration between Olin College and North Hill a senior living community in Needham, MA. The Fund provides support for Olin students and faculty members to undertake projects and research related to aging.
