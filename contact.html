Contact Us

We would love to hear from you! Please send any questions, comments, or suggestions to aging@olin.edu or through the below form. We are especially interested in your ideas of projects – often the best opportunities to help come from people outside of a research setting. Let us know your thoughts!


Current suggestions include:

  • a chair that a mobility impaired individual can push into a table
  • a case to keep hearing aids open (and the battery disengaged) when not in use
  • a way to carry gardening tools while using a cane

Note: solutions may already exist that we have yet to discover
