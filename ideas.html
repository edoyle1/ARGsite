Your Ideas

We need your help. Often times the best opportunities for projects come from people outside of a research group setting. This is your place to tell us about an annoyance faced that you would like to go away - either for yourself or for someone you know.

Current suggestions include:
a chair that a mobility impaired individual can push into a table
a case to keep hearing aids open (and the battery disengaged) when not in use
a way to carry gardening tools while using a cane

Note: solutions may already exist that we have yet to discover

Things suggested for which we have stumbled across commercial solutions:
annoyance presented (terms to google for a solution)
a way to make buttoning buttons easier (“Button Hook”)
a way to hold up a book for reading without using the hands (“Book Holder” “Book Stand” “Bed and Chair Book Holder”)
a way to make opening jars easier (“Jar Opener”)
